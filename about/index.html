About me

Hi, I’m Jessica, a senior at Smith College majoring in engineering and computer science.

My hometown is Beijing, China. I have a deep passion for Chinese food and cuisine.

I enjoy being both a researcher and an adventurous explorer in my life. As a researcher, I am interested in applying my engineering knowledge to promote community healthcare. My current research project focuses on Effects of Ear Canal Geometry on Wideband Acoustic Immittance Measurements under the supervision of Professor Susan Voss at Smith College.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I love travelling. I have visited several great places by myself.

Swimming has been a part of my life since the age of 5, and I’m a proud member of the Smith College Synchronized Swimming Club.

I love being a cheerleader! It is much more than standing in front of a crowd. It needs time, trust, and determination.

I am a member of the Smith Equestrian team.

I also work as a stage lighting designer. I’m a huge musical theatre fan.

I usually stakeboarding to school.

I like board games. especially poker. I enjoy visiting art galleries with my friends.
